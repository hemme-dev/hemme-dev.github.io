# WeiqiVision - Support

Welcome to the WeiqiVision support page, your personal Go / Weiqi / Baduk gym.

## What is WeiqiVision?

WeiqiVision is an innovative application designed to help you improve your Go skills through advanced visualization exercises. The app allows you to:

- Sharpen your skills with innovative visualization exercises
- Boost your shape recognition
- Strengthen your ability to memorize and spot patterns

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How do I get started with WeiqiVision?

1. Download the app from your device's app store (Google Play Store, App Store, or Mac App Store)
2. Open the application
3. Select the type of exercise you want to practice
4. Start training!

### Who is this app for?

WeiqiVision is perfect for:

- Beginner Go players who want to develop fundamentals
- Intermediate players looking to improve pattern recognition
- Advanced players who want to maintain and refine their visualization abilities

### What types of exercises does the app offer?

The app focuses on:

- Board visualization exercises
- Shape and pattern recognition
- Move sequence memorization
- Quick identification of tactical situations

### Does the app work offline?

Once downloaded, the app can work offline. However, an internet connection is recommended to receive updates and new content.

### Does the app contain ads?

Yes, WeiqiVision contains advertisements that help keep the app free.

## Common Issues and Solutions

### The app won't start

1. Check that you have the latest version of the app installed
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure you have enough storage space on your device
4. If the problem persists, uninstall and reinstall the application

### Exercises won't load properly

1. Check your internet connection
2. Force close the app and reopen it
3. Clear the app cache from your device settings (Android: App Settings, iOS/macOS: Offload/Reinstall App)
